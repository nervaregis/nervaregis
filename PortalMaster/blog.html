      Emerging factions

      Hello there everyone! It's time for another blog! Today we'll be going over the new factions in Aftermath. Due to the wish to keep things mostly vanilla and try to follow through with the time progression since we're trying to keep the amount of new factions somewhat low, however there are some cases where they are needed to fill a roleplay or gameplay niche. The two factions we'll be going over today are the Red Sails, a Bretonian based pirate faction and the Sons of Ymir, a Sigma / Omicron based unlawful group dedicated to going after the GMG and Corsairs.
